## Services at our Med Spa in Las Vegas

### 1\. Facial and Skin Rejuvenation

Experience our luxurious facials and skin rejuvenation procedures, which are designed to revitalize your complexion and give you glowing skin. We offer a range of facial options from a traditional customized facial for gentle maintenance to HydraFacial and Diamond Glow for a more transformative treatment.

### 2\. Botox and Fillers

Injectable treatments are a great way to restore youth and create facial balance. Our Nurse Practitioner Injector will carefully perform Botox and Filler injections to enhance your natural beauty and help you age gracefully. While maintaining natural results is our top priority, we always tailor the treatment to your preferences.

### 3\. Sofwave

Looking for face-lift-like results with no downtime? Sofwave is your answer! This is the latest technology to stimulate collagen and lift the skin using ultrasound waves. With its unique design, we can penetrate into the dermis to stimulate collagen that will continue to grow over the next 3 months. Because of its non-invasive nature, there is little to no visible downtime, making it a very convenient option for a busy lifestyle.

### 4\. Thread Lift

For a more dramatic skin lifting effect, our advanced injector may recommend a Thread Lift. This is a good option for those who prefer a minimally invasive option rather than surgery to lift and tighten certain areas of the skin.

### 5\. Emsculpt Neo and Emsella

Sculpt and tone your body with Emsculpt Neo. We offer the latest advanced treatment to non-invasively decrease fat while simultaneously building muscle. Trustest by celebrities and athletes alike, Emsculpt Neo has been taking over the body contouring and fitness industry. Kick start your fitness or maintain your strength with Emsculpt Neo today. Emsella is the FDA cleared non-invasive option to repair and strengthen the pelvic floor to improve urinary incontinence and sexual wellness. Combine Emsculpt Neo with Emsella to treat your core and pelvic floor together.

### 6\. Other services

Explore our selection of additional treatments, which include acne targeted chemical peels, microneedling, laser vein reduction, hair loss therapy, laser hair removal, and tattoo removal. Our team of advanced aestheticians and nurse practitioner injector are well educated and experienced with the best treatments available, ensuring you can achieve the results you’ve been looking for!
